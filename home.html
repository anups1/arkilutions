ArKilutions

Providing architectural solutions for your environment


We at Arkilutions have the sole intention to design elegant, beautiful and functional environments. We specialise in architectural, interior, landscape, product and artefact designing. Designing better every day, so as to capture the beauty of nature and containing it in your environment is our aim. Take a look at the projects we have undertaken under the Projects tab. Also, don't miss out on the Wall-Art tab which is an exclusive technique provided by Arkilutions.




Residential

  • Furnished Flat

    The complete flat was furnished by Arkilutions. The bed was custom made to suit the needs of our client. Lighting was provided so as to keep the room appropriately illuminated.

  • Bedroom Interiors

    The color of the bedroom changed depending on the lighting. In the day the color seems to be of a lighter shade, whereas in the night it seems to be dark. This was a little play in the color and lighting to suit the needs of our client

  • Passageway lights

    The allignment was chosen so as to make the passageway look sofisticated. Also, a wall-art was provided in the adjoining hall, which can be seen in the far left of the picture.

Offices

  • Office Interiors

    The complete office interiors was design to promote productivity. The Tables and chairs are custom made. The inclination of the table was decided with engineering precision, so as to reflect light at accurate angles on the desk.

  • Custom-made Chairs

    The chairs were designed to give an antiquey look to the furniture. The arm-rest was designed so as to provide proper comfort. There is a play with the table inclination too.

  • Custom-made Sofa

    The color of the sofa was chosen to match the entire scheme of the office. As we designed the complete office we could choose the fabric, the wood and the craftsmen providing best services to our clients.

Commercial

  • Shop Interiors

    This was a watch shop which was re-designed completely by Arkilutions to provide the right ambience. The colors of the ceiling was chosen so as to reflect light more on the showcase.

  • Showcase

    This showcase was built from ground up by Arkilutions and was given a small window to put artefacts to attract customers walking on the streets.

  • Tea Trader

    An ongoing project for a tea trader. Here we are working with different styling elements rather than the conventional. Innovation is an aspect we focus on.

Wall-Art

This is a techinique where a trained artist paints directly on the wall with washable paints. This gives an aesthetic sense to the walls, removing dull; monotonous colors and filling it with a vibrant story. This technique has been well-received and applauded by our clients.

  • The Buddha

    Our client wanted to place an idol and we provided it with the perfect background. It gives a somewhat three-dimentional look and builds up the scene of the meditating buddha.

  • The Fields

    The fake wall was created to display this artwork. This provides the aesthetic look promised. Our client wanted a scenario of a field. This wall-art was made to create that theme.

  • The Circles

    This was painted on a vertical piece of furniture which could be extended horizontally to provide a desk. After extension too the art remains complete. Visit our page for more.
